Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer? ▼

We offer virtual consultations, pet care advice, and expert guidance for different types of pets.

What is the best food for my dog? ▼

The best food depends on your dog’s breed, age, and health. Consult our Dog Owners page for more details.

How can I transition my cat to a new diet? ▼

Gradually introduce the new food over 7-10 days by mixing it with the old food.

What type of housing is best for small pets? ▼

Guinea pigs, hamsters, and rabbits require spacious cages with proper ventilation and safe bedding.

How can I book a consultation? ▼

You can book a consultation through our Contact Us page.
