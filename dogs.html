Dog Care & Training

Dog Care & Training

Dogs need proper nutrition, exercise, and training to live happy lives.

Nutrition

Puppies need high-protein food, while adult dogs need a balanced diet to maintain a healthy weight.

Exercise & Play

Dogs should get 30-120 minutes of exercise daily, depending on breed and size.

Health & Vaccinations

Core vaccinations protect against rabies, parvovirus, and distemper. Always consult a vet.
