Dog Page Content

The domestic dog is a highly social animal with over 300 recognized breeds. Studies show that owning a dog can reduce blood pressure and improve mental well-being.

Diet & Exercise

Puppies should be nursed for two months, then gradually transitioned to solid food. Feeding guidelines:

  • 3–6 months: Three meals per day
  • 6 months–1 year: Two meals per day
  • 1 year and older: One meal per day

Dogs require 30–120 minutes of exercise per day depending on breed and size.

Grooming & Handling

Regular grooming helps prevent matting and skin issues. Bathing frequency depends on breed and activity level. When carrying small dogs, support their chest and back legs.

Vaccinations & Medications

Core vaccinations include protection against rabies, hepatitis, and parvovirus. Other vaccines depend on environmental exposure.
